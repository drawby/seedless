Our Story

Seedless is a holding company in the emerging marijuana market. With technology for the marijuana business as it's core focus, Seedless develops technology products and makes strategic investments in both “touching” and “non-cannabis touching” industries. This diversified approach allows Seedless to sustain any volatility from changes in law regarding cannabis production and/or distribution.
